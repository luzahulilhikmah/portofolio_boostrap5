Kim Junkyu

Singer | Idol

About Me

South Korean singer Kim Junkyu, also known as Junkyu. He is a member of the boy group TREASURE.

YGE announced its future goals in a notification that was published on their official website on January 6. The two units, TREASURE and MAGNUM, were combined into a single group known as simply TREASURE as well as reorganizing as a 12-member group.

Kyu's Projects

He took part in the MIXNINE reality survival show in 2017. After placing 35th in the episode 10, he was eliminated.

The teaser video and poster for TREASURE's August 7 debut were released by YG Entertainment on July 20. TREASURE's debut single album, THE FIRST STEP: CHAPTER ONE, was made available on August 7.

On January 21, YG said that TREASURE would be releasing THE FIRST STEP: TREASURE EFFECT, their first Japanese album. This album would feature the Japanese translation of all 12 songs from their first studio album, plus BEAUTIFUL.

The debut single "MOVE" by TREASURE's new five-member unit T5 and its music video were released on June 28 at 6 p.m. KST. Notably, Junkyu from TREASURE co-wrote the song's melody with producer DEE.P, and Junkyu wrote all of the lyrics by himself.

On July 28th, TREASURE finally returned with the second full-length album, 'REBOOT' displaying a more sophisticated and dapper appearance.
